## Fundusz ZDOLNI Project Internship - Photos

Here you can find photos documenting my work on the project I developed at Makerspace@UW as part of the Fundusz ZDOLNI scholarship program, which also funded my work on this project. I am very grateful for that support.

What I gained the most from these workshops was practical experience with tools, hands-on making, and planning my work more realistically. Even though I did not manage to finish the project completely, I learned a lot throughout the process.

That is all from me, feel free to explore the photos, both the serious project ones and the more humorous ones too :)
